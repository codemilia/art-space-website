Hi! This is me, Cath, in my natural habitat, the art studio. Whether it’s pottery, painting or paper crafting, my soul feels at ease. I like to say that art is as important to our health as sports. Therefore, I’d like to provide you a safe space, where you can get to know your inner artist and create free of expectations!

My lovely art studio is located 20min from the city center in a calm environment, which makes it easy to switch off. The studio is equipped with everything you need, so you don’t have to bring any materials or tools!
